Welcome!

In this exercise, you will be asked to carry out a number of tasks on a website.

On the right side of the page, you will see a blue box with a scenario and the current task you need to complete. Like the one pictured below (and the one you probably just moved).

To complete each task, you need to navigate through the website until you feel you have completed the task and then select 'Task Complete'.

If you are having difficulty finishing the task, you can select 'Abandon Task'.

If the box is in the way, use the controls on the left to move it around or hide it. If your mouse gets stuck, just reload the page.

Each set of tasks are followed by a few questions.

Some Background Information

The idea with this product is to create a platform that enables teachers like you to use popular technology to engage with students between the ages of 13-17 in the class room.

This piece of the product allows you to setup a live video stream and live conversation. In the class this tool would allow you to connect experts, other teachers

Our aim is to help you craft an interesting, relevant and engaging experience based on a curriculum. We want to help students understand their impact, the problems they are helping solve, and reflect on everything you learned together. We would also like to faciliate access to professionals and industry leaders for career advice and inspiration.

Missions refer to the boat trips some of the student go on to get hands on experience with ocean science.

The goal of this test is to understand if we have created a useful product for you. Honest and constructive feedback is important and enables us to design a better product for you.

Please only carry out the test once.

Are you ready?

Please remember — we are not evaluating you, we are evaluating the website. All inputs are anonymous. You will be testing a a prototype, which means that it is not fully functional. But hopefully it will give you an idea of how this would be a good or a bad thing in teaching ocean science.
