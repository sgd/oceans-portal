This event is upcoming

Mission

We need your help identifying local Invasive Species! These creatures thrive in the absence of their native predators and have the potential to drastically alter habitat, rendering it inhospitable for native species.

Location

We will be exploring and working in Placentia Bay, Newfoundland. A Location we believe is high in Invasive Species.

Careers

Experience first hand what it's like to be a Marine Biologist solving real problems. You will work hands on with our 2 resident scientists -- take measurements, photos and video to help them understand the green crab invasion.

Technology

Using the same robots that the professionals do, you will get the opportunity to explore the ocean floor with an ROV. You will also get to measure and learn about the ocean conditions using other professional level equipment.
